Standard Operating Procedures

How Each Discount Works

A step-by-step breakdown of how we apply Multi-Policy Bundling, Good Payer Rewards, and EFT discounts to your policy.

I
Multi-Policy Bundling (Auto + Home/Renters)
Objective: Combine Auto with Home, Condo, or Renters insurance to save the client up to 10% or more on both policies.
1.1
Identification: Review existing policyholder accounts (monoline) for potential to cross-sell.
1.2
Quoting Process: Generate a home/renters quote for current auto clients.
1.3
Value Proposition: Highlight that bundling reduces paperwork (one bill) and provides a "multi-policy" discount (up to 20% in some cases).
1.4
Implementation: If policies do not renew simultaneously, discuss pro-rating the new policy to align with the existing one for immediate savings.
II
Good Payer Reward (Consistent Payment History)
Objective: Reward clients who have not had a late billing fee in the 12 months prior to their renewal processing date.
2.1
Qualification: Verify that the policyholder has a clean, on-time payment record for the past 12 months.
2.2
Application: The Good Payer discount is generally applied automatically at renewal.
2.3
Promotion: Proactively identify clients reaching 12 months of on-time payments to notify them of upcoming rate decreases at renewal.
III
EFT Discount & Autopay Setup (Required Focus)
Objective: Apply a discount for setting up Automatic Fund Transfers (EFT) and encourage monthly, automated payments to secure lower rates.
3.1
Promotion: Explain that EFT reduces administrative costs, which are passed on to the customer through a discount.
3.2
Enrollment — Online: Direct the customer to Farmers.com → Log In → "Set up Autopay".
3.3
Enrollment — Mobile App: Navigate to Bills → Bill Details → Set up automatic payments through the Farmers mobile app.
